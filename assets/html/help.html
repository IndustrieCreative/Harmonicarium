Short Help

This program currently under development.

Google Chrome (or Chromium) browser is required.

MIDI OUT feature is unavailable at the moment.

In order to listen an OUTPUT SOUND there is the “Built-in Synth”. Its ON/OFF checkbox must be flagged.

If you have a MIDI keyboard you can connect and chose it from “Input ports”.

If you have NO physical MIDI keyboard you can use the “Virtual MIDI Controller” with your mouse or pressing the “qwerty” and “asdfgh” rows on your PC keyboard. The range is very limited due to the current JavaScript keyboard (Qwerty Hancock) that must be modded or replaced.

Since this program is still in beta and its UI must be totally replaced, I cannot write an exhaustive guide at the moment. The functioning in a few words is as follows.

OVERVIEW

You have two keyboard layers:

  • Fundamental Tones (FT): the red keys.
  • Harmonic Tones (HT): the blue keys.

You can play the Harmonic Series on the blue keys.

You can change their fundamental by pressing the red keys; the harmonics on blue keys are instantly recalculated.

The program starts with the Harmonic Series of a C3 (130.81Hz). This is the Fundamental Mother

KEYMAPS

The position of the FTs and HTs on the keyboard is set on the “Controller Keymap”. You can create your own controller keymap.

In the /keymaps/_mapping-tools/ folder there is the "keymapping-tool.xlsx" that can be used to generate keymaps. Other maps can be found on the /keymaps/n-edx/ . You can download the "offline" app.

FUNDAMENTAL TONES

The FT are like a palette of frequencies available to generate harmonics. The default FT are 12 tone equally tempered (12-TET or 12-EDO). In the Fundamental Tones box you can edit the FT tuning. At the moment only equal temperaments are available.
